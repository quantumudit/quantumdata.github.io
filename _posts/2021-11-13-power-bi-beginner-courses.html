Power BI is undoubtedly an amazing business intelligence tool that we currently have in the market and with growing demands for data analytics; it becomes really important to know this particular tool.

When I was a beginner in Power BI; I struggled to find the proper content from where should I start?

Although we have a lot of content on Power BI present online, still beginners struggle to find the appropriate content to get started.

So, here I will point out a couple of courses that I think could be helpful for anyone getting started with Power BI

Prerequisites

It is okay to begin Power BI afresh but, having a basic knowledge of Pivot Tables is helpful.
Here are two great course by Chris Dutton available on Linkedin Learning:

  • Excel Pivot Tables (Mastering Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts)
  • Excel Pivot Tables (Real-World Case Studies)

Power BI Beginner Friendly Courses

I must say that Power BI has a steep learning curve and there are several things that we need to understand before becoming a pro in it; however, I will discuss these in some other posts.
Here are two good courses available for free over YouTube by Enterprise DNA that I would recommend to everyone:

  • Ultimate Beginners Guide to Power BI
  • Ultimate Beginners Guide to DAX

There are many other fantastic & beginner friendly courses available today but, I've decided not to overwhelm this particular post by providing links for too many courses; however, I'll keep on posting my choice of course recommendation in future posts.
